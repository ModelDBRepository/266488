The models and methods for the paper:

Wybo WAM, Torben-Nielsen B, Nevian T, Gewaltig MO (2019) Electrical Compartmentalization in Neurons. Cell Rep 26:1759-1773.e7
http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.celrep.2019.01.074

are available on the web through this data sharing declaration in the paper:

"Code for the derivation of NETs from biophysical neuron models can be found online as part of the NEural Analysis Toolkit (NEAT, https://github.com/WillemWybo/NEAT).

We provide scripts to reproduce results from this paper (https://github.com/WillemWybo/Electrical_compartmentalization_in_neurons).

To run these scripts, NEAT's NEURON extension is also needed (https://github.com/WillemWybo/NEAT_NEURON_extension)."
